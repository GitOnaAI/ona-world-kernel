# Terms and Conditions — template placeholder

Ona World Kernel is a game **template**. It ships without terms of service,
because the terms belong to the game you build with it — your rules, your
jurisdiction, your legal entity.

Before shipping a game built on this kernel:

1. Write your own terms and conditions (who operates the service, account
   rules, acceptable use, moderation policy, content ownership, disclaimers,
   limitation of liability, governing law).
2. Replace this file and the served page at `public/terms.html`.

The kernel's source code license is [MIT](LICENSE) and is not affected by
whatever terms you adopt for your hosted service. Sample game content is
attributed in [CREDITS.md](CREDITS.md).
